Defination of H2O:

Water (H2O) is a transparent, tasteless, odorless, and nearly colorless chemical
    substance that is essential for all known forms of life. It covers about
    71% of Earth's surface, primarily in oceans, rivers, and lakes. Water
    exists in three states: liquid, solid (ice), and gas (vapor). It plays a
    crucial role in biological processes, climate regulation, and sustaining
    ecosystems. The human body is composed of about 60% water, making it vital
    for hydration, digestion and health.
